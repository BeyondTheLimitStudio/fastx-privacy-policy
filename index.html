By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

FastX respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it. We design our application to function with minimal data collection.

X (formerly Twitter) Login Data and Cookies

Our application allows you to log into your X (formerly Twitter) account via an embedded secure browser to download age-restricted, protected, or private content.

WE DO NOT COLLECT, STORE, MONITOR, OR TRANSMIT YOUR X (FORMERLY TWITTER) USERNAME, PASSWORD, OR PERSONAL INFORMATION TO OUR SERVERS.

When you log in, the authentication tokens (cookies) are strictly stored locally and securely on your own device. These cookies are only used to authenticate requests directly from your device to X (formerly Twitter) to retrieve video links. They are never sent to our servers, and we have no access to them. You can delete these cookies at any time by logging out from the Profile screen in the app.

Downloaded Content

Any videos, images, or content you download using FastX are stored directly on your device's local storage. We do not track, monitor, or keep records of what you download.

4. Security of Data

The security of your data is extremely important to us. Because we do not collect your personal information or login credentials, there is virtually no risk of server-side data breaches from our end. All sensitive processes (like Twitter authentication) happen locally on your device using Android's built-in secure WebView and encrypted preferences.

However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data locally, we cannot guarantee absolute security against malware on your own device.
