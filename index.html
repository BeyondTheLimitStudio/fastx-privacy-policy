1. Information We Do NOT Collect

We are transparent: FastX does not operate any back-end server that receives, stores, or processes your personal data. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or contact information
  • Your X (formerly Twitter) username or password
  • Your download history or browsing activity within the app
  • Your location data

2. X (formerly Twitter) Login & Session Cookies

How it works: Some content on X (formerly Twitter) requires an authenticated session to access. FastX provides an optional in-app browser login so you can authenticate directly with X. When you do so:
  • Your credentials are entered directly into X's own login page — we never see them.
  • The resulting session cookies (authentication tokens) are stored exclusively and securely on your own device using Android's built-in storage.
  • These tokens are used only to make requests from your device to X's servers to retrieve media links. They are never transmitted to our servers.
  • You can remove them at any time by tapping Log Out on the Profile screen.

3. Advertising — Google AdMob

FastX displays ads provided by Google AdMob to support ongoing development. AdMob may collect and use the following to serve relevant ads:

  • Android Advertising ID (AAID)
  • General device information (model, OS version)
  • General usage signals (clicks, impressions)

This data is collected and processed by Google under their own privacy policy. You can review it here: https://policies.google.com/privacy. You can opt out of personalised ads at any time from your device's Settings → Google → Ads.

4. Permissions We Require

  • Internet Access — to fetch media and serve ads.
  • Storage / Media Files — to save downloaded content to your gallery.
  • Post Notifications — to show download progress in the notification tray.
  • Foreground Service — to keep downloads running reliably in the background.

5. Data Security

Because we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is no server-side risk of a data breach affecting you. Session cookies stored locally on your device are managed by Android's secure storage. We recommend keeping your device up to date and using a screen lock for general security.

The app may contain links to external sites (e.g. Play Store, our support email). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their individual privacy policies.
